Welcome, fellow traveler.
Before anything else, my name is Alberto and I'm glad you're here.

                
For a long time, I've felt the pull of a new horizon. Like many of you, I've been a passenger in the digital age, observing from the shore. But recently, I decided to build my own vessel and I've embarked on a new journey as an Indie developer, a blogger, and a scripter — learning to navigate the vast, open waters of creation on my own terms.

                
Why Lumaenaut?

                
Lumænaut ( /ˈluːmɪnɔːt/ )
Etymology: From Latin lumen ("light") + Greek nautes ("sailor, traveler").
Meaning: A traveler of light.

                
It's a word I crafted for this very voyage. It comes from lumen — the measurement of visible light — and naut — meaning traveler or voyager. So, literally, a traveler of light. (And yes, I used the 'æ' character just because I love the way it looks; it feels like a small spark in the word itself).

                
This website is my ship's log. It's the space where I document the constellations I navigate by.

                
Here, we'll explore the intersection of three of my deepest passions:

                
Digital Nomadism: The art of building a location-independent life. Where the code meets the road.

                
Computer Science: The engine room. The logic, the structures, the beautiful complexity that makes our modern world possible.

                
Videogames: The stories we inhabit. The art, the script, and the pure joy of play.

                
This is more than just a blog. It's an invitation.

                
I invite you to follow along as I navigate the currents of code, the landscapes of new cities, and the infinite worlds inside our screens. There will be discoveries, there will be storms, and hopefully, there will always be light.

                
Welcome to the journey.

                
Pack your bags. The adventure starts now.

                
Alberto Del Valle

                
Indie Developer | Coder
